Who Needs a Commercial License?
You need a commercial license if you intend to:
Integrate AetherAI into a proprietary (closed-source) product or service
Distribute AetherAI as part of a commercial offering without releasing your source code
Use AetherAI in a SaaS product where you do not wish to open-source your modifications
White-label AetherAI under a different brand in a commercial context
Use AetherAI in any way that conflicts with AGPL v3 obligations
You do NOT need a commercial license if you:
Use AetherAI for personal, non-commercial purposes
Run AetherAI internally within your organisation with no external distribution
Build and distribute open-source products using AetherAI under a compatible license
Are a registered non-profit organisation using AetherAI for humanitarian purposes

Mandatory Conditions for All Commercial Licenses
Regardless of tier, all commercial licensees must:
Preserve attribution — Display "Built on AetherAI by H. Sharma" in their product's
documentation or About page. This requirement survives beyond H. Sharma's lifetime.
Comply with the Ethics Charter — Commercial licensees are bound by ETHICS.md.
Violations result in immediate license revocation.
Report security vulnerabilities — Commercial licensees who discover security
vulnerabilities in AetherAI must report them to the security team within 48 hours.
Not sublicense — Commercial licenses cannot be sublicensed, sold, or transferred
to third parties without explicit written approval from the Governance Council.
Cooperate with audits — The Governance Council reserves the right to audit
commercial licensees for Ethics Charter compliance with 30 days notice.
